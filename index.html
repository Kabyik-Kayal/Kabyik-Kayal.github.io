About Me

Hey! I'm a Data Science undergrad at IIT Madras who spends most of my time building AI models and trying to make them actually work in production (spoiler: it's harder than it looks). When I'm not debugging code or wrestling with deployment pipelines, you'll catch me grinding games, watching anime, or going down mythology rabbit holes. Balance, right? I'm that person who can't stop asking "but why does it work that way?" which has led to a pretty chaotic portfolio ranging from Anime Recommendation to Voice Cloning Apps. If it involves data and sounds interesting, I'm probably building it. Right now, I'm obsessed with Large Language Models and MLOps. I write about what I learn because honestly, I wish someone had explained this stuff to me when I started. Based in Kolkata, always building something at the intersection of creativity and code. Want to talk about AI research, collaborate on a project, or debate which anime arc is the best? Let's connect!
